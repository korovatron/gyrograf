How To Use Gyrograf

Choose a ring piece, track, and wheel size from the controls.

Pick an Ink colour and Pen width, then drag a wheel hole to draw curves.

Use pinch-to-zoom on touch, mouse wheel to zoom on desktop, and drag empty space to pan.

Hide Gear toggles ring and wheel visibility so you can present the clean trace.

Reset View recentres and refits the current diagram.

Exporting

Export PNG saves the current diagram as a tightly cropped PNG. On mobile it opens the native share sheet; on desktop it downloads the file.

About Gyrograf

Gyrograf is a digital simulation inspired by the classic Spirograph drawing toy system.

The original Spirograph was developed by British engineer Denys Fisher and became an iconic creative maths-and-art tool from the 1960s onward.

This app recreates that classic ring-and-wheel drawing experience in a browser, including inner/outer tracks, different wheel tooth counts, and pen-hole tracing to produce hypotrochoid and epitrochoid-style curves.
